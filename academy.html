Course Description

As a graphic designer, your skill is always in demand by businesses and organisations seeking
to create and maintain their visual imageries.
Through this course you will learn fundamental design concept that will enable you to design attractive graphic contents such as flyers (for online and prints) company brochures and brand
identities.

You will learn and master CorelDraw and Adobe Photoshop to enable you create beautiful and professional designs.
